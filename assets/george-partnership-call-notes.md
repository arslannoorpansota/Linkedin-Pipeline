## PARTNERSHIP STRUCTURES to float
| Model | How it works | When it fits |
|---|---|---|
| **Referral** | He intros the client, you contract directly, he takes a referral % | He wants to stay hands-off after the intro |
| **Advisory-on-top** | He stays retained as strategist, you deliver underneath | He wants ongoing client ownership |
| **White-label** | You deliver under his brand, invisible to client | He's selling a full "strategy + delivery" package |

Don't force one — ask which he prefers, then align.
